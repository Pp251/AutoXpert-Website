The design style should feel similar to Apple-style product pages: large hero sections, smooth scrolling storytelling, parallax effects, subtle animations, floating UI elements, and a clean premium aesthetic.

Brand Personality:
Futuristic, intelligent, trustworthy, minimal, high-tech automotive innovation.

Color Palette:
Use a dark modern palette with deep charcoal, dark navy, and subtle electric accents (cyan/blue glow). Use soft gradients and glassmorphism effects to create a premium feel.

Typography:
Use large bold headlines similar to Apple product pages. Clean sans-serif fonts with strong hierarchy.
